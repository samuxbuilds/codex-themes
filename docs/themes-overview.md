### 7. Accessibility (10+ themes)

WCAG-compliant themes optimized for accessibility.

**Included:**
- Accessible Dark (high contrast, bright cyan)
- Accessible Light (bold colors, clear text)
- Dyslexia Friendly (sans-serif, good spacing)
- Low Blue Dark (reduces blue light, eye care)
- Protanopia Friendly (red-green colorblind)
- Deuteranopia Friendly (red-green colorblind, alt palette)

**Usage**: Better eyesight, reduced strain, colorblind support.

**Best for**: Accessibility advocates, anyone with visual needs.

---

### 8. Spectrum (144+ themes)

Full 360° hue sweep from red to violet, with 4 styles each:
- Dark (standard)
- Light (light variant)
- Vibrant (saturated, bold)
- Matte (desaturated, muted)

**Generated as:**
- Every 10° hue (0°, 10°, 20°... 350°)
- 4 style variations × 36 hues = 144 themes

**Usage**: Scientific approach to color. Find your exact hue.

**Best for**: Designers experimenting with color theory.

---

### 9. Duo-tone (80+ themes)

Two-color combinations using complementary and split-complementary color theory.

**Examples:**
- Fire & Ice (red + blue)
- Sunset & Ocean (orange + blue)
- Spring & Dusk (green + purple)
- Tropical (mint + pink)
- Earth & Sky (brown + blue)
- Berry & Mint (magenta + green)
- Gold & Violet (gold + purple)

**How it works:**
- Accent color: First hue
- Sidebar/secondary: Second hue
- Creates visual harmony through contrast

**Usage**: Professional, sophisticated looks.

**Best for**: Designers, agencies, premium projects.

### 14. Soft (60+ themes)
